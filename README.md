# My Days

**My Days** is an Android app that turns any date into a rich snapshot of history, astronomy, and culture — powered by Google's Gemini AI.

Tap a date on the calendar and get:
- Historical events and a curated quote for that day
- Lunar phase, sunrise/sunset, and astronomical data
- International observances and cultural highlights
- Content in your device's language
