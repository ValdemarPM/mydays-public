Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how My Days ("the app") handles information when you use it. My Days is a calendar and historical-events app that uses Google's Gemini AI to generate content about selected dates.

1. Information We Process

When you tap on a date, the app sends the following to the Google Gemini API:

  • The selected calendar date
  • Your device language preference

No personal information (name, email, location, device identifiers, or account data) is collected, stored, or transmitted by the app.

2. How the Data Is Used

The date and language are sent solely to generate AI-produced historical and astronomical information about that date. The response is displayed to you and is not stored or logged by the app.

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the Google Gemini API to generate content. Data sent to this API is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. The app does not use any other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

4. Data Storage

My Days does not maintain any servers that store user data. No account is required and no personal data is retained after you close the app.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "last updated" date.
